Han started his career as a blockchain security engineer at Suho, one of Korea's top blockchain infrastructure companies, working directly on smart contract vulnerabilities and on-chain threats. Where most builders focus on features first, he starts with the question of what structure will actually hold up.

That foundation naturally grew into a perspective that covers the whole product. When he co-founded FanX Protocol, a DEX on Chiliz Chain, he took on the PO role and owned everything from security architecture to user experience. His standard is that a product needs to be both secure and genuinely enjoyable to use, and that those two things should never have to trade against each other.
